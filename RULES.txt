

				RULES ARE SIMPLE:

							* Player1 will use the ARROW KEYS to move and the M KEY to plant bombs.
							
							* Player2 will use the W,A,S,D KEYS to move and the F KEY to plant bombs.
							
							* Your goal is to get out of the maze first.
							
							* If a player runs by a bomb, his controls will be shuffled for 20 moves.
							
							* After you plant a bomb, you can't plant another one unless you press
										15 times on the key that plants bombs.
							
							* Each player will be represented using the initial of his/her name.
										
						
						
																											GOOD LUCK GETTING OUT OF THE MAZE
						
				
				
				Press any key to start the game ...
